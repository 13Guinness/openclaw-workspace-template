## When to Use

✅ **USE when:**
- Tracking product/startup ideas
- Ranking ideas by potential
- Collaborating with team
- Market gap analysis
- Generating research prompts

❌ **DON'T use when:**
- Solo brainstorming (overkill)
- Already have project management tool
- Need full product roadmap (too simple)

## Key Features

- Multi-user collaboration
- Weighted scoring (passive potential, feasibility, revenue)
- Market gap tracking
- AI research prompt generation
- Real-time filtering/sorting

## Scoring System

3 factors (1-10 each):
1. Passive Income Potential
2. Feasibility
3. Revenue Potential

Total: Max 30 points
